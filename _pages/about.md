Hi! I am **Yixian Jiang**, an MSc Cybersecurity student at the
[University of Glasgow](https://www.gla.ac.uk) (School of Computing Science), supervised by Prof. [Shahid Raza](https://shahidraza.net/). I received my BEng in
Information Security from [North China University of Technology](https://en.ncut.edu.cn/index.htm).

My work sits at the intersection of **Cybersecurity** and **AI**. I am broadly interested in:

- **Trustworthy Agentic AI Systems**
- **LLM Security**
- **AI for IDS**
- **IoT Security**

Currently, I am working on the Identity and Privilege 
Abuse in the Agentic AI communication protocols, [the top-3 threat](https://genai.owasp.org/resource/owasp-top-10-for-agentic-applications-for-2026/) for the  Agentic AI.
