Hi! I am **Yixian Jiang (蒋毅贤)**, an MSc Cybersecurity student at the
[University of Glasgow](https://www.gla.ac.uk) (School of Computing Science), and a Research
Assistant at the State Key Laboratory of Complex and Critical Software Environments (SKLCCSE),
Beihang University, advised by Prof. Xianglong Liu and Prof. Aishan Liu. I received my BEng in
Information Security from North China University of Technology (NCUT).

My work sits at the intersection of **security** and **trustworthy AI**. I am broadly interested in:

- **Secure and Trustworthy Agentic AI Systems**
- **LLM Security**
- **AI-enabled Cyber Defence**
- **IoT / ICS Security**

I like grounding security questions in concrete systems and working prototypes — from
machine-learning-based intrusion detection to memory-safe tooling in Rust.

See my [publications](/publications/), [projects](/projects/), and [CV](/cv/), or feel free to
[get in touch](/contact/).
